## 学习策略

Awareness

在学习之前，先询问自己（元认知：metacognition）：

- How am I feeling before I even start learning?
- How do my feelings change as I start to move through the learning?
- What might be confusing me right now?

But awareness goes beyond just understanding your thoughts. It's also about **managing your emotions and actions**.

## 时间管理

Relationship with Time

意识到自己和时间的关系，目标和时间的关系。培养一个习惯平均要 66 天（根据一项调查研究 [How Long Does it Take to Form a Habit? Backed by Science.](https://jamesclear.com/new-habit#:~:text=On average%2C it takes more,to form a new habit.)，最少 21 天，最长 254 天）

Pomodoro Technique

1. Decide on the task to be done.
2. Set a timer for 25 minutes and work on the task.
3. When the timer rings, take a short break of 3-5 minutes. Make a checkmark on a piece of paper or other note-taking space to mark one completed pomodoro. 
4. Repeat steps 1-3 for a total of four pomodoro cycles.
5. After four pomodoros, take a longer break (15–30 minutes), reset your checkmark count to zero, then go back to step 1.
